Manual FNVEdit Editing Recommended:

  • Run FNVEdit through MO2
  • Right-click in the Module Selection pop-up and click Select None
  • Check the box adjacent to Improved Traits.esp then hold down left shift and click OK
  • Wait for the message Background Loader: finished to appear in the right pane
  • Expand the Improved Traits.esp header in the left pane and the Perks header within it
  • Right-click on One In a Million in the left pane and select Remove
    • Removes a trait that was too overpowered
  • Input 0200351A into the FormID search box in the top left and hit enter
  • In the right pane, right-click on the box adjacent to the empty box labeled Icon and select Add
  • Double-click on the box adjacent to the new ICON box and paste in interface\icons\pipboyimages\perks\perk_four_eyes.dds
    • ×
    • Restores the missing icon for the Four Eyes trait
  • Save and exit

- Balances/changes many vanilla traits to make the choice between them more difficult and adds two more traits inspired by classic Fallout
